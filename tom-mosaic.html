Tomato Mosaic-Solution

Pesticide Name:

Imidacloprid

Estimated Cost:

Imidacloprid:Rs.450-Rs.600 per acre.

Dosage:

Imidacloprid:0.3ml per litre water.

How to Use:

Mix pesticide in sprayer tank.

Spray on leaves to control insect vectors.

Repeat after 7-10 days if needed.

Causes

Caused by Tomato Mosaic virus.

spread through infected tools,hands,and plant contact.

Advantages of Pesticides

Protects plant growth.

Reduces virus spread.
