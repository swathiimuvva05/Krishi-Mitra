Dosage

Emamectin Benzoate:0.4g per litre of water.

Chlorantraniliprole:0.3ml per litre of water.

Spinosad:0.3ml per litre of water.

Flubendiamide:50-60g in 200L water.

Novaluron:200ml in 200L water

Thiodicarb:400g in 200L water.

Profenofos:400-600 ml in 200L water.

Quinalphus:400ml in 200L water.

Cypermethrin:200ml in 200L water.

Deltamethrin:200-250ml in 200L water.

Chlorpyrifos:400ml in 200L water.

How to Use:

Fill 10-15 litres of water in sprayer.

Add Pesticde according to recommended dose.

Spray on flowers and cotton bolls.

Repeat spray after 10-12 days if needed.

Causes

The female moth lays eggs on Cotton squares,flowers,and bolls.

After hatching,the larve enter the cotton bolls.

The larve feeds on cotton seeds and fiber inside the boll.

Beacuse they stay inside the boll,they are difficult.

Advantages of Pesticides

Reduces boll damage.

Improves cotton fibre quality.

Prevents yield loss.

Helps farmers get better market price.
