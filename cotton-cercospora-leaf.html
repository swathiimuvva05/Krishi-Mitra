Cercospora Leaf Spot-Solution

Pesticide Name:

Mancozeb

Carbendazim

Copper Oxychloride

Estimated Cost:

Mancozeb:Rs.250-Rs.350 per kg.

Carbendazim:Rs.300-Rs.400 per 100g.

Copper Oxychloride:Rs.300-Rs.500 per kg.

Dosage

Mancozeb:2-3g per litre of water.

Carbendazim:1g per litre of water.

Copper Oxychloride:3g per litre of water.

How to Use

Fill 10-15 litres of water in a sprayer.

Add fungicide according to recommended dose.

Spray on both sides of leaves.

Repeat spray after 10-12 days if needed.

Causes

The disease is caused by the fungus Cercospora gossypina.

Dense planting and poor air circulation.

High humidity and rainfall.

Advantages of Pesticides

Prevents leaf spot spread.

Reduces early leaf fall.

Maintains healthy plant growth.
