Damping Off-Solution

Pesticide Name:

Mancozeb

Carbendazim

Estimated Cost:

Mancozeb:Rs.250-Rs.350 per acre.

Carbendazim:Rs.300-Rs.400 per acre.

Dosage:

Mancozeb:2g per litre water.

Carbendazim:1g per litre water.

How to Use:

Mix fungicide in water.

Drench nursey soil around seedlings.

Repeat after 7-10 days if needed.

Causes

Caused by fungi like Pythium,Rhizoctonia solani.

Spread through infected soil and water.

Advantages of Pesticides

Improves survival rate in nursery.

Prevents plant death.

Protects young seedlings.
