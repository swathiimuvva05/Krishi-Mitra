Leaf Roller-Solution

Pesticide Name:

Chrlorantraniliprole/Cartap Hydrochloride

Chrlorantraniliprole:Estimated Cost:

Cartap Hydrochloride:Rs.250-Rs.350 (100ml bottle)

How to Use:

Mix 0.4ml Chrlorantraniliprole in 1 litre of water and spray on the crop

OR

Mix 2 grams Cartab Hydrochloride in 1 litre of water

Spray evenly on affected leaves

Repeat spray after 10-12 days if needed

Causes of Leaf Rollers:

Attack of leaf roller insect(caterpillar)

Warm and humid weather conditions

Dense crop growth in the field

Lack of proper pest management

Advantages of Chrlorantraniliprole/Cartap Hydrochloride

Controls leaf roller insects

Prevents leaf damage and rolling

Protects the crop from futher pest attack

Helps improve plant growth and yield
