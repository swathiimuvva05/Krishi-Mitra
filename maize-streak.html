Streak Disease-Solution

Pesticide Name:

Imidacloprid

Estimated Cost:

Rs.450-Rs.600 per acre.

Dosage:

0.3ml per litre water.

How to Use:

How to Use:

Mix fungicide in water.

Spray on leaves to control insect vectors.

Repeat after 7-10 days if needed.

Causes

Caused by virus Leafhopper.

Spread through wind and humid weather.

Advantages of Using Pesticides

Controls insect pests.

Reduce virus spread.

Improves crop growth.
