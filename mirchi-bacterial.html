Bacterial Leaf Spot-Solution

Pesticide Name:

Copper Oxychloride

Streptomycin

Estimated Cost:

Copper Oxychloride:Rs.350-Rs.500 per acre.

Streptomycin:Rs.400-Rs.600 per acre.

Dosage:

Copper Oxychloride:2g per litre water.

Streptomycin:0.5g per litre water.

How to Use:

Mix pesticide in sprayer tank.

Spray evenly on leaves.

Repeat after 7-10 days.

Causes

Caused by bacteria Xanthomonas Campestris.

Advantages of Pesticedes

Controls bacterial infection.

Prevents leaf damage.

Improves plant health.
