Hairy Caterpillar-Solution

Pesticide Name:

Chlorpyrifos

Estimated Cost:

Around Rs.400-Rs.600 per acre.

Dosage:

2ml per litre water.

How to Use:

Mix pesticides with water in sprayer.

Spray on leaves where caterpillars are present.

Causes

Larvae feed on leaves and tender plant parts.

Advantages of Pesticides

Quickly kill caterpillars.

Protect plant leaves and crop growth.
