Curl Leaf of Mirchi-Solution

Pesticide Name:

Imidacloprid

Thiamethoxam

Acetamiprid

Estimated Cost:

Imidacloprid: Rs.450-Rs.600 per acre.

Thiamethoxam:Rs.500-Rs.700 per acre.

Acetamiprid:Rs.350-Rs.550 per acre depending on the brand.

Dosage:

Imidacloprid:0.3ml per litre of water.

Thiamethoxam:0.25g per litre of water.

Acetamiprid:0.2g per litre of water.

How to Use:

Mix the pesticide in water properly.

Spray on both sides of leaves.

Repeat after 7-10 days if needed.

Causes

Leaf cur; disease is mainly cause by Chilli Leaf Curl Virus.

Caused by Virus spread mainly by whitefly.

High temparature and dry weather.

Advantages of Pesticides

Controls whiteflies and other sucking pests.

Reduces spread of leaf curl diseases

Helps chilli plants grow healthy and produce more yield.
