Anthracnose Wilt-Solution

Pesticide Name:

Carbendazim

Mancozeb

Estimated Cost:

Carbendazim:Rs.300-Rs.400 per acre.

Mancozeb:Rs.250-Rs.350 per acre.

Dosage:

Carbendazim:1g per litre water.

Mancozeb:2g per litre water.

How to Use:

Mix pesticide in sprayer tank.

Spray on leaves and fruits.

Repeat after 7-10 days.

Causes

Caused by fungus like Colletotrichm.

High Humidity and dry weather.

High rainfall increae infection.

Advantages of Pesticides

Prevents fruit rot.

Improves fruit quality.

Increases market value.
