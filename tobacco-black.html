Black Shank-Solution

Pesticide Name:

Metalaxyl

Estimated Cost:

Metalaxyl:Rs.400-Rs.600 per acre.

Dosage:

Metalaxyl:1g per litre of water.

How to Use:

Mix fungicide in water.

Apply by soil drenching near roots.

Causes

Caused by fungus Phytophthora nicotianae.

Advantages of Pesticides

Controls root and stem infection.

Prevents plants death.
