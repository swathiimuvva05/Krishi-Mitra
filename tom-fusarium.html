Fusarium Wilt-Solution

Pesticide Name:

Carbendazim

Metalaxyl

Estimated Cost:

Carbendazim:Rs.300-Rs.400 per acre.

Metalaxyl:Rs.400-Rs.600 per acre.

Dosage:

Carbendazim:1g per litre.

Metalaxyl:1g per litre of water.

How to Use:

Mix fungicide in water.

Apply by soil drenching near roots.

Repeat after 7-10 days if needed.

Causes

Caused by fungus Fussarium oxysporum.

Spread through infected soil plant residues.

Advantages of Pesticides

Controls Fungal infection.

Prevents plant wilting and death.
