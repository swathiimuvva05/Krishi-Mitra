Common Rust-Solution

Pesticide Name:

Propiconazole

Estimated Cost:

Rs.500-Rs.700 per acre.

Dosage:

1ml per litre water.

How to Use:

Mix fungicide in water.

Spray on leaves showing rust spots.

Repeat after 10 days if needed.

Causes

Caused by fungus Puccinia sorghi.

Spread through wind and humid weather.

Advantages of Using Pesticides

Prevents Rust damage.

Protects maize leaves and yield.
