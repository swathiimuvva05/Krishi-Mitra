Charcoal Rot-Solution

Pesticide Name:

Carbendazim

Estimated Cost:

Rs.300-Rs.400 per acre.

Dosage:

1g per litre water.

How to Use:

Mix Pesticide with water.

Apply by soil drenching near roots.

Causes

Caused by fungus Macrophomina phaselina.

Occurs mostly in hot and dry conditions .

Advantages of Pesticides

Controls root infection.

Protects plant growth yield.

Prevents plant death.
