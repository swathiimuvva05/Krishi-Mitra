Gram Pod Border-Solution

Pesticide Name:

Emamectin Benzoate

Estimated Cost:

Rs.500-Rs.700 per acre.

Dosage:

0.4g per litre water.

How to Use:

Mix pesticde with water.

Spray on flowers and pods.

Repeat after 7-10 days if needed.

Causes

Larvae feed on flowers and pods.

Advantages of Pesticides

Kills caterpillar quicky.

Protects pods and seeds.
