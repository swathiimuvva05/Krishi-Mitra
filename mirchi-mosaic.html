Mosaic Disease-Solution

Pesticide Name:

Imidacloprid

Acetamiprid

Estimated Cost:

Imidacloprid:Rs.450-Rs.600 per acre.

Acetamiprid:Rs.350-Rs.550 per acre.

Dosage:

Imidacloprid:0.3g per litre of water.

Acetamiprid:0.2-0.3g per litre of water.

How to Use:

Fill 10-15 litres of water in a sprayer.

Add the pesticde according to the recommended dose.

Spray on both sides of leaves to control insect pests.

Repeat spray after 7-10 days if pests remain.

Causes

Caused by plant viruses such as Cucumber mosaic virus.

Spread mainly sucking insects like Aphid and Whitefly.

The Virus enters the plants when insects feed on the leaves.

Advantages of Pesticedes

Controls insect pests that spread the virus.

Reduces spread of mosaic disease.

Improves plant growth.
