Blast Disease-Solution

Pesticide Name:

Tricyclazole/Isoprothiolane

Estimated Cost:

Tricyclazole:Rs.250-Rs.350 (100g Packet)

Isoprothiolane:Rs.300-Rs.400 (200ml bottle)

How to Use:

Mix 1 gram Tricyclazole in 1 litre of water

Spray on the crop

OR

Mix 1.5ml Isoprothiolane in 1 litre of water

Spray evenly on affected leaves

Repeat spray after 10-12 days if required

Causes of Blast Diseases:

Fungal Infection in rice plants

High Humidity and moisture in the field

Cool weather conditions

Excess nitrogen fertilizer

Advantages of Using Tricyclazole/Isoprothiolane

1.Controls blast fungus effectively

2.Protects leaves and grains from damage

3.Improves plant health and growth

4.Helps increase paddy yield
