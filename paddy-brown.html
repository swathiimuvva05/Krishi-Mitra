Brown Spots-Solution

Pesticide Name:

Mancozeb/Carbendazim

Estimated Cost:

Mancozeb:Rs.250-Rs.350 per 1 kg Packet

Carbendazim:Rs.150-Rs.250 per 1kg packet

How to Use Carbendazim:

Mix 1 gram Carbendazim in 1 litre of water

Spray on the crop

Spray 2-3 times at 10 day interval

How to Use Mancozeb:

Mix 2 grams Mancozeb in 1 litre of water

Spray evenly on affected leaves

Causes of Brown Spots:

Fungal infection in rice plants

Low soil fertility

High humidity and moisture

Poor crop management

Advantages of Using Mancozeb/Carbendazim

1.Controls brown spot diseases effectively

2.Protects leaves from further infection

3.Improves plant health and growth

4.Helps increase paddy yield
