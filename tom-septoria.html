Septoria Leaf Spot-Solution

Pesticide Name:

Mancozeb

Chlorothalonil

Estimated Cost:

Chlorothalonil:R.400-Rs.600 per acre.

Mancozeb:Rs.250-Rs.350 per acre.

Dosage:

Mancozeb:2g per litre of water.

Chlorothalonil:2g per litre water.

How to Use:

Mix fungicide in 10-15L water.

Spray evenly on leaves and stems.

Repeat after 7-10 days if needed.

Causes

Caused by fungus Septoria lycopersici.

Spread through rain splash,wind,and infected plant debris.

Advantages of Pesticides

Improves plant health and yield.

Prevents leaf damage.
