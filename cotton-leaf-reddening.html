Leaf Reddening-Solution

Pesticide Name:

Magnesium Sulphate

Potassium Sulphate

Imidacloprid

Estimated Cost:

Magnesium Sulphate:Rs.40-Rs.60 per kg.

Potassium Sulphate:Rs.70-Rs.100 per kg.

Imidacloprid:Rs.700-Rs.900 per 250ml bottle.

Dosage:

Magnesium Sulphate:10g per litre of water.

Potassium Sulphate:5g per litre of water.

Imidacloprid:0.3ml per litre of water.

How to Use:

Take 10-15 litres of water in a sprayer.

Add Magnesium Sulphate or Potassium Sulphate in recommended quality.

Spray uniformly on both sides of leaves.

Repeat after 10-15 days if required.

Causes:

Mainly due to nutrient deficiency

Attack of suckking pests like Aphid,Jassid,and whitefly.

Drought or moisture stress.

Advantages of Using Pesticide:

Leaves return to healthy green color.

Improves photosynthesis and plant growth.

Better boll formation and cotton yield.

Prevents crop stress and quality loss.
