Bacteria Leaf Blight-Solution

Pesticide Name:

Streptocycline/Copper Oxychloride

Estimated Cost:

Streptocycline:Rs.120-Rs.180 (10g Packet)

Copper Oxychloride:Rs.300-Rs.400 (500g packet)

How to Use:

Mix 0.1 gram Streptocycline in 1 litre of water

Spray on the crop

OR

Mix 2 grams Copper Oxychloride in 1 litre of water

Spray evenly on affected leaves

Repeat spray after 10-12 days if required

Causes of Bacteria Leaf Blight:

Bacterial infection in rice plants

Heavy rainfall and strong winds

High humidity and warm temparature

Excess nitrogen fertilizer

Advantages of Using Streptocycline/Copper Oxychloride

1.Controls bacterial infection effectively

2.Protects leaves from further infection

3.Improves plant health and growth

4.Helps increase paddy yield
