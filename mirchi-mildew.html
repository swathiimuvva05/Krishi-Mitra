Powdery Mildew-Solution

Pesticide Name:

Wettable Sulphur

Estimated Cost:

Rs250-Rs.350 per acre.

Dosage:

2g per litre water.

How to Use

Mix fungicide with water.

Spray on infected leaves.

Causes

Caused by fungus Leveillula taurica.

Advantages of Pesticedes

Controls fungal infection.

Protects leaves.

Improves plant growth.
