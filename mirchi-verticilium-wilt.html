Verticilium Wilt-Solution

Pesticide Name:

Carbendazim

Metalaxyl

Estimated Cost:

Carbendazim:Rs.300-Rs.400 per acre.

Metalaxyl:Rs.400-Rs.600 per acre.

DosageL

Carbendazim:1g per litre water.

Metalaxyl:1g per litre water.

How to Use:

Fill 10-15 litres of water in a sprayer or bucket.

Add the fungicide according to dosage.

Mix the solution well

Apply by soil drenching near the roots of infected plants.

Causes

Caused by fungus Verticilium dahliae.

The fungus lives in the soil and enters the plant through roots.

It blocks the water-conducting vessels of plant.

Advantages of Pesticedes

Prevents plant wilting and death.

Protects the root system.

Improves plant growth.
