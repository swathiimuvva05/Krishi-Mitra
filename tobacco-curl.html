Leaf Curl-Solution

Pesticide Name:

Thiamethoxam

Estimated Cost:

Around Rs.450-Rs.650 per acre.

Dosage:

0.25g per litre of water.

How to Use:

Spray pesticide on leaves to control whiteflies.

Causes

Virus spread by Whiteflies.

Advantages of Pesticides

Controls Insect vector.

Reduces virus spread.
