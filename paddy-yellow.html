Yellow Leaves-Solution

Pesticide Name:

Urea/Zinc Sulphate

Estimated Cost:

Rs.400-Rs.600 per acre

How to Use:

Mix 1kg urea in 100 litres of water and spray evenly on Leaves Repeat after 10 days if needed

Apply Urea:25-30kg per acre

Apply Zinc sulphate:10kg per acre mixed with soil

Causes of Yellow Leaves:

1.Nitrogen deficiency in the Soil

2.Poor soil fertility

3.Excess water or poor drinage

4.Pest or disease attack

Advantages of Using Urea/Zinc Sulphate

1.Restores green leaf color

2.Improves plant growth

3.Helps in chlorophyll production

4.Prevents nutrient deficiency
