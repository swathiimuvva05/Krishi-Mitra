Aphids-Solution

Pesticide Name:

Imidacloprid

Estimated Cost:

Around Rs.400-Rs.600 per acre.

Dosage:

0.3ml per litre water.

How to Use:

Spray pesticide on leaves to control that spread the virus.

Spray pesticide on affected leaves.

Causes

Aphids suck plan sap.

Advantages of Pesticides

Controls sap-sucking pests.

Improves plant growth.
