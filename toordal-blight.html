Phytopthora Blight-Solution

Pesticide Name:

Metalaxyl

Estimated Cost:

Around Rs.400-Rs.500 per acre.

Dosage:

1g per litre water.

How to Use:

Spray fungicide on affected plants.

Can also apply soil drenching near root zone.

Causes

Soil fungus spread by rainwater and high moisture.

Advantages of Pesticides

Controls Fungal infection.

Prevents plant death and yield loss.
