Curl Leaves-Solution

Pesticide Name:

1.Imidacloprid

2.Thiamethoxam

3.Acetamiprid

Estimated Cost:

Imidacloprid-Rs.700-Rs.900 per 250ml bottle.

Thiamethoxam-Rs.600-Rs.800 per 100g.

Acetamiprid-Rs.500-Rs.700 per 100g.

Dosage:

Imidacloprid:0.3ml per litre of water.

Thiamethoxam:0.25g per litre of water.

Acetamiprid:0.2g per litre of water.

How to Use:

Take 10-15 litres of water in a sprayer.

Add pesticide according to the recommended dose.

Spray on both sides of leaves.

Spray early morning or evening.

Causes:

Caused by virus.

Mainly spread by whitefly.

High Temparature and humid conditions increase the disease.

Advantages of Pesticides:

Reduces whitefly population.

Prevents Spread of leaf curl virus.

Better fiber quality and farmer profit.
