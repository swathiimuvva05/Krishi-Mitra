Downy Mildew-Solution

Pesticide Name:

Metalaxyl

Estimated Cost:

Metalaxyl:Rs.400-Rs.600 per acre.

Dosage:

1g per litre of water.

How to Use:

Mix fungicide in water.

Spray evenly on leaves.

Repeat after 7-10 days if needed.

Causes

Caused by fungus Peronosclerospora sorghi.

Spreads through wind,rain,and infected crop residues.

Advantages of Pesticides

Controls fungal infection.

Protects plant growth yield.
