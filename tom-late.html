Late Blight-Solution

Pesticide Name:

Metalaxyl

Mancozeb

Estimated Cost:

Metalaxyl:Rs.400-Rs.600 per acre.

Mancozeb:Rs.250-Rs.350 per acre.

Dosage:

Metalaxyl:2g per litre of water.

Mancozeb:1g per litre of water.

How to Use:

Mix fungicide in water.

Spray on leaves and fruits.

Repeat after 7-10 days if needed.

Causes

Caused by fungus Phytophthora infestans.

Spreads quickly in cool and humid weather.

Advantages of Pesticides

Controls rapid disease spread.

Protects fruits from rotting.
