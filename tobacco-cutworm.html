Cutworm-Solution

Pesticide Name:

Chlorpyrifos

Estimated Cost:

Around Rs.400-Rs.600 per acre.

Dosage:

2ml per litre water.

How to Use:

Mix pesticides with water in sprayer.

Spray pesticides near plants from damage.

Causes

Caterpillars feed on plants stems at night.

Advantages of Pesticides

Quickly kill caterpillars.

Protect young plants from damage.
