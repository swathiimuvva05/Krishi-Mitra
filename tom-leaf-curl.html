Leaf Curl-Solution

Pesticide Name:

Imidacloprid

Acetamiprid

Estimated Cost:

Imidacloprid:Rs.450-Rs.600 per acre.

Acetamiprid:Rs.350-Rs.550 per acre.

Dosage:

Imidacloprid:0.3ml per litre water.

Acetamiprid:0.2g per litre water.

How to Use:

Mix pesticide in sprayer tank.

Spray on both sides of leaves.

Repeat after 7-10 days if needed.

Causes

Caused by whitefly.

Advantages of Pesticides

Control Whiteflies.

Reduces virus spread.
