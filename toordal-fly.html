Pod Fly-Solution

Pesticide Name:

Dimethoate

Estimated Cost:

Around Rs.350-Rs.500 per acre.

Dosage:

1.5ml per litre water.

How to Use:

Spray pesticde on pods and flowers.

Causes

Larvae develop inside pods and feed on seeds.

Advantages of Pesticides

Prevents seed damage.

Improves grain quality.
