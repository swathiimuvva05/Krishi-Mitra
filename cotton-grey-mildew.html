Grey Mildew-Solution

Pesticide Name:

Carbendazim

Mancozeb

Copper Oxychloride

Estimated Cost:

Carbendazim:Rs.300-Rs.400 per 100g.

Mancozeb:Rs.250-Rs.350 per kg.

Copper Oxychloride:Rs.300-Rs.500 per kg.

Dosage:

Carbendazim:1g per litre of water.

Mancozeb:2-3g per litre of water.

Copper Oxychloride:3g per litre of water.

How to Use:

Fill 10-15 litres of water in a sprayer.

Add fungicide according to the recommended dose.

Spray on both sides of cotton leaves.

Repeat spray 10-12 days if needed.

Causes

Grey mildew is caused by the fungus Remularia areola.

High humidity.

Poor air circulation in the field.

Advantages of pesticides

Prevents leaf damage and early leaf fall.

Improves photosynthesis in plants.

Helps in better boll formation.
