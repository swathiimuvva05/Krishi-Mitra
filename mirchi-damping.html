Damping off Mirchi-Solution

Pesticide Name:

Carbendazim

Mancozeb

Estimated Cost:

Carbendazim:Rs.300-Rs.400 per acre.

Mancozeb:Rs.250-Rs.350.

Dosage:

Carbendazim:1g per litre water.

Mancozeb:2g per litre of water.

How to Use:

Mix pesticide in water according to the recommended dose.

Drench the soil around seedlings or spray nursery beds.

Repeat application after 7-10 days if needed.

Causes

Caused by soil fungi such as Pythium,Rhizoctonia solani,and Fusarium.

Occurs in nursery beds with poor drianage or exceesive moisture.

Dense sowing of seeds increase risk.

Advantages of Using Pesticides

Protets seedlings from rotting.

Ensures healthy plant establishment.

Reduces losses in nursery stage.
