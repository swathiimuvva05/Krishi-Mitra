Leaf Spot-Solution

Pesticide Name:

Mancozeb

Estimated Cost:

Mancozeb:Rs.250-Rs.350 per acre.

Dosage:

Mancozeb:2g per litre of water.

How to Use:

Mix fungicide with water in sprayer.

Spray evenly on infected leaves.

Repeat after 7-10 days if needed.

Causes

Caused by fungal pathogens spread through rain splash.

Advantages of Pesticides

Stops spread of fungal disease.

Protects foliage and plant growth.
