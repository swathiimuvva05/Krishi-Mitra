Dry Leaves-Solution

Pesticide Name:

Potassium Chloride(MOP)/Urea

Estimated Cost:

Rs.200-Rs.300 per 1kg

Urea:Rs.270-Rs.300 per 45kg Bag

How to Use:

Mix 1-2 grams of potassium fertilizer in 1 litre of water and spray on leaves

Apply urea to soil or spray diluted solution on the crop

Spray 2 times at 10-12 day interval

Causes of Dry leaves:

Potassium deficiency in Soil

Lack of water or drought conditions

High temparature and heat stress

Fungal or bacterial diseases

Advantages of Potassium Chloride

Helps recover dry and weak leaves

Improves plant strength and growth

protects plants from stress and diseases
