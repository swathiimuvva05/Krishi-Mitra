Boll Rot-Solution

Pesticide Name:

Mancozeb

Carbendazim

Copper Oxychloride

Estimated Cost:

Mancozeb:Rs.250-Rs.350 per kg.

Carbendazim:Rs.350-Rs.400 per 100g.

Copper Oxychloride:Rs.300-Rs.500 per kg.

Dosage:

Mancozeb:2-3g per litre water.

Carbendazim:1g per litre water.

Copper Oxychloride:3g per litre water.

How to Use:

Take 10-15 litres of water in sprayer.

Add the fungicide according to the recommended dose.

Spray during morning or evening.

Repeat spray after 10-12 days if necessary.

Causes

Boll rot is caused by different fungi like Aspergillus,Rhizopus,Fusarium.

High humidity and rainfall.

Damage by insects.

Water stagnation in fields.

Advantages of pesticides

Prevents rotting of cotton bolls.

Improves cotton fibre quality.

Reduces yield loss.
