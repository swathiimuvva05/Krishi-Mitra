Root Rot of Mirchi-Solution

Pesticide Name:

Carbendazim

Copper Oxychloride

Estimated Cost:

Carbendazim:Rs.300-Rs.400 per acre.

Copper Oxychloride:Rs.350-Rs.500 per acre.

Dosage:

Carbendazim:1g per litre of water.

Copper Oxychloride:2g per litre of water.

How to Use:

Mix pesticde with water.

Apply by soil drenching near roots.

Repeat after 10-12 days.

Causes

Caused by funfi like Fusarium.

Waterlogging and poor drinage increase disease.

Advantages of Pesticides

Prevents root damage.

Improves plant growth and yield.
