Root Rot of Cotton-Solution

Pesticide Name:

Carbendazim

Copper Oxychloride

Trichoderma

Estimated Cost:

Carbendazim:Rs.300-Rs.400 per 100g.

Copper Oxychloride:Rs.300-Rs.500 per kg.

Trichoderma:Rs.250-Rs.400 per kg.

Dosage:

Carbendazim:1g per litre of water.

Copper Oxychloride:3g per litre of water.

Trichoderma:2.5kg mixed with farmyard manure per acre.

How to Use:

Mix fungicide with water according to recommended dose.

Pour the solution near the root zone of infected plants.

Apply the morning or evening.

Causes

Root rot is mainly caused by soil fungi such as Rhizoctonia solani and Fusarium.

Water logged soil.

Continuos cotton cultivation in the same field.

Advantages of Pesticides

Stops root decay and plant death.

Improves root growth and nutrient absorption.

Reduces crop loss in patches.
