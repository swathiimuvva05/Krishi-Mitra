Powdery Mildew-Solution

Pesticide Name:

Hexaconazole

Estimated Cost:

Around Rs.400-Rs.500 per acre.

Dosage:

1ml per litre of water.

Mix Funficde with water.

Spray evenly on leaves and plant canopy.

Repeat after 7-10 days if needed.

Causes

caused by fungal infection during cool and humid weather.

Advantages of Pesticides

Controls fungal growth.

Protects leaves and improves plant health.
