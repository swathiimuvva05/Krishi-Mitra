Damping off-Solution

Pesticide Name:

1.Carbendazim

2.Thiram

3.Metalaxyl

Estimated Cost:

Carbendazim-Rs.300-Rs.400 per 100g.

Thira-:Rs.250-Rs.350 per 100g.

Metalaxyl-Rs.400-Rs.600 per 100g.

Dosage:

Carbendazim:2g per kg seed.

Thiram:3g per kg seed.

Metalaxyl:about 2g per kg seed or soil treatment.

How to Use:

1.Take the required cotton seeds.

2.Add reommended fungicide dose.

3.Mix seeds well so the chemical coats all seeds.

4.Dry in shade for 30 minutes.

Then show the seeds in the field.

Causes:

Caused by soil fungi such as Pythium,Rhizoctonia,Fusarium.

Occurs mostly in wet soil and poor drainage.

Overwatering and very dense sowing increase the disease.

Using infected or untreated seeds also causes it.

Advantages of Using Pesticides:

Improves seed germination percentage.

Protects young cotton seedings.

Ensures uniform plant population in the field.

Leads to better crop growth and higher yield.
