Budworm-Solution

Pesticide Name:

Emamectin Benzoate

Estimated Cost:

Around Rs.500-Rs.700 per acre.

Dosage:

0.4g per litre water.

How to Use:

Spray pesticide on leaves and buds.

Causes

Larvae feed on tender plant parts.

Advantages of Pesticides

Quickly controls caterpillars.
