Verticilium Wilt-Solution

Pesticide Name:

Carbendazin

Copper Oxychloride

Trichoderma

Estimated Cost:

Carbendazin:Rs.300-Rs.400 per 100g.

Copper Oxychloride:Rs.300-Rs.500g per kg.

Trichoderma:Rs200-Rs.400g per kg.

Dosage:

Carbendazin:1g per 1 litre of water.

Copper Oxychloride:3g per litre of water.

Trichoderma:2.5gkg mixed with farmyard manure per acre.

How to Use:

Mix fungicide in water according to recommended dose.

Apply by soil drenching near plant roots.

Use trichderma with organic manure and apply to soil.

Practice crop rotation and remove infected plants.

Causes:

This disease caused by the soil fungus Verticilium dahliae.

Cool and moist conditions.

Infected soil or crop residues.

Advantages of Using Pesticides

Prevents plant wilting and death.

Improves root and plant health.

Reduce spread of soil-borne fungus.
