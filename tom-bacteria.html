Bacterial Wilt-Solution

Pesticide Name:

Streptomycin

Copper Oxychloride

Estimated Cost:

Streptomycin:Rs.400-Rs.600 per acre.

Copper Oxychloride:Rs.350-Rs.500 per acre.

Dosage:

Streptomycin:0.5g per litre water.

Copper Oxychloride:2g per litre water.

How to Use:

Mix Pesticide with water.

Apply by soil drenching near roots.

Causes

Caused by bacteria Ralstonia solanacearum.

Spread through infected soil and water.

Advantages of Pesticides

Controls bacerial infection.

Prevents plant death.
