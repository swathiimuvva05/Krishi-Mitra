Leaf Spot-Solution

Pesticide Name:

Mancozeb

Estimated Cost:

Mancozeb:Rs.250-Rs.350 per acre.

Dosage:

Mancozeb:2g per litre of water.

How to Use:

Mix fungicide in water.

Spray evenly on leaves and stems.

Repeat after 7-10 days if needed.

Causes

Caused by fungi pathogens spread by rain and wind.

Advantages of Pesticides

Improves plant health and yield.

Prevents spread leaf infection.
