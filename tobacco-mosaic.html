Mosaic Disease-Solution

Pesticide Name:

Imidacloprid

Estimated Cost:

Around Rs.400-Rs.600 per acre.

Dosage:

0.3ml per litre water.

How to Use:

Spray pesticide on leaves to control that spread the virus.

Remove infected plants.

Causes

Caused by virus spread through infected plant sap,tools,and hands.

Advantages of Pesticides

Reduce virus spread.

Protects healthy plants.
