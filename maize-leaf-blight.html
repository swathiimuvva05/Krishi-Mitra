Maize Leaf Blight-Solution

Pesticide Name:

Mancozeb

Propiconazole

Estimated Cost:

Mancozeb:Rs.250-Rs.350 per acre.

Propiconazole:Rs.500-Rs.700 per acre.

Dosage:

Mancozeb:2g per litre of water.

Propiconazole:1ml per litre of water.

How to Use:

Mix pesticide in 10-15 litres of water.

spray evenly on maize leaves.

Repeat after 7-10 days if needed.

Causes

Caused by fungus Exserohilun turcicum.

Spread through wind,rain,and infected crop residues.

Advantages of Using Pesticides

Prevents leaf damage.

Improves plant health and yield.
