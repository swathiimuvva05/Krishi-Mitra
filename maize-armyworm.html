Fall ArmyWorm-Solution

Pesticide Name:

Emamectin Benzoate

Spinetoram

Estimated Cost:

Emamectin Benzoate:Rs.500-Rs.700 per acre.

Spinetoram:Rs.700-Rs.900 per acre.

Dosage:

Emamectin Benzoate:0.4g per litre water.

Spinetoram:0.5ml per litre water.

How to Use:

Mix fungicide in 15 litres of water.

Spread directly into the leaf whorl(center of plant).

Repeat after 7-10 days if pests continues.

Causes

Caused by armyworm larvae feeding on maize leaves.

Spreads quickly in warm and humid weather.

Advantages of Pesticides

Kills armyworm larvae quicky.

Protects maize leaves and ears.

Prevents heavy crop loss.
