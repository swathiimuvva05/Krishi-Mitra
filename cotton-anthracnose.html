Anthracnose Wilt-Solution

Pesticide Name:

Carbendazim

Mancozeb

Thiram

Estimated Cost:

Carbendazim:Rs.300-Rs.400 per 100g.

Mancozeb:Rs.250-Rs.350 per kg.

Thiram:Rs.250-Rs.350 per 100g.

Dosage:

Carbendazim:1g per litre of water.

Mancozeb:2-3g per litre water.

Thiram:3g per kg seed.

How to Use:

Seed Treatment

Mix Thiram with cotton seed at recommended dose.

Coat seeds evenly before sowing.

Spraying Method

Mix Carbendazin or Mancozeb with water.

Spray on leaves and bolls using sprayer.

Repeat spray after 10-12 days if needed.

Causes:

This disease is Caused by the fungus Colletotrichum gossypii.

Warm and humid weather.

Infected seeds.

Continuos cotton cultivation.

Advantages of Pesticeds

Protects seedlings and cotton bolls.

Reduces spread of fungi infection.

Improves cotton fibre quality.
