Corn Smut-Solution

Pesticide Name:

Propiconazole

Estimated Cost:

Rs.500-Rs.700 per acre.

Dosage:

1ml per litre water.

How to Use:

Spray fungicide on maize plants when symptoms appear.

Remove and destroy infected galls.

Causes

Caused by fungus Ustilago maydis.

Spread through wind and humid weather.

Advantages of Pesticides

Controls fungal infection.

Protects plant growth yield.
