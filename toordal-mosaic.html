Sterility Mosaic-Solution

Pesticide Name:

Dicofol

Estimated Cost:

Around Rs.400-Rs.600 per acre.

Dosage:

2ml pe litre of water.

How to Use:

Mix fungicide in 15L water.

Spray evenly on leaves and plant tops.

Repeat after 7-10 days if mites appear again.

Causes

Caused by virus Eriphyid Mite.

Advantages of Pesticides

Controls mites that spread the virus.

Reduces disease spread in the field.
